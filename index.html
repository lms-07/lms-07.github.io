About Me

Hello! I am an incoming Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, The University of New South Wales (UNSW Sydney), fortunate to be supervised by Dr. Dong Gong, Prof. Erik Meijering, and Prof. Lina Yao. Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering and M.Eng. degree in Artificial Intelligence from Shandong University (SDU) in 2024 and 2021, respectively. For my master's research, I was luckily advised by Prof. Gongping Yang and as a member of TIME Lab.

Research

My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Currently, I am interested in Foundation Models, especially Vision-Language Models as well as Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning. Previously, I explored Remote Sensing Image Analysis in depth with a focus on Hyperspectral Image Classification and Pan-sharpening.
